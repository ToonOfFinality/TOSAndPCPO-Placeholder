# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Toontown Modernized (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our game and Discord integration features.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect only the information necessary to operate the game and Discord-linked features:
- Game account ID
- Discord user ID
- Discord username
- OAuth tokens required to update Linked Roles metadata

We do **not** collect:
- Discord passwords
- Email addresses
- Payment information
- Direct messages or server content

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Link your game account with your Discord account
- Verify your identity for Linked Roles
- Assign in-game or Discord roles
- Maintain account security and prevent abuse

## 3. Data Storage
Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized systems required to operate the game.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

## 4. Cookies
Our OAuth login system uses temporary cookies to store your game account ID during the Discord login process. These cookies are not used for tracking or advertising.

## 5. Third-Party Services
Our Services use the Discord API for authentication and Linked Roles. Your use of Discord is subject to Discord’s own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Our Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Services means you accept the updated policy.

## 8. Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through our Discord server or support channels.
